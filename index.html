About us

Burger King is a global fast-food chain renowned for its flame-grilled burgers and commitment to quality. Established in 1954 in Miami, Florida, the brand has grown to become one of the world's largest fast-food franchises, with thousands of locations across the globe. At Burger King, our mission is to provide high-quality, flame-grilled burgers with a taste that sets us apart. We focus on delivering fast, convenient, and satisfying meals to our customers, ensuring they have a memorable dining experience every time they visit.
